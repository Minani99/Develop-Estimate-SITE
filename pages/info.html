What is a Static Page?

A static page is a fixed webpage that doesn't change dynamically. It is fast and simple. Common use cases include landing pages, portfolios, and blogs.

What is Hosting?

Hosting is a service that stores your website's files and makes them accessible online. It is essential for making your site available to users.
